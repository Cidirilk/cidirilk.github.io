CIDIRILK — if you can't pronounce it, read it backwards

Signal work tracing the fragile line between control and surrender.

Kliridis Charalampos (CIDIRILK) builds radio shows, performances, and collaborations that value presence over perfection. Since 2017 the project has been a loop of learning, unlearning, and rediscovering meaning through sound.

LiveSets hosts the residency broadcasts, ChaCha expands the research as a duo, and every past event leaves a fragment for the next story. Expect glitch, care, and curiosity stitched together.
