CIDIRILK Sessions

if you can't pronounce it, read it backwards

Signal work tracing the fragile line between control and surrender.

Kliridis Charalampos (CIDIRILK) crafts radio shows, live performances, and sonic experiments that embrace presence over perfection. Since 2017, the project has been a continuous loop—learning, unlearning, and rediscovering meaning through sound.

LiveSets hosts the residency broadcasts. ChaCha expands the research as a duo. Every past event leaves fragments for the next chapter. Expect glitch, care, and curiosity stitched together with intention.
