Human After All. Looking for Something Real.

In a world full of noise, Cidirilk is an attempt to listen a little closer. Since 2017, the project has followed curiosity through sound, collecting fragments of experience, emotion, and change along the way.

Not every question needs an answer. Not every journey needs a destination. Sometimes, what we find was never what we were looking for.
